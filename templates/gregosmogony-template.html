About Greg

Greg Osmogony is a digital artist and creative technologist specializing in generative art and algorithmic design. With a background in mathematics and computer science, he explores the intersection of computational processes and aesthetic expression.

His work focuses on creating beautiful visualizations from mathematical concepts, particularly in areas of chaos theory, fractal geometry, and complex dynamical systems. Through code, he seeks to reveal the hidden patterns and emergent beauty that exist within mathematical structures.
